Privacy Policy

Goldstone Properties · Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Goldstone Properties (“we,” “us,” or the “Company”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the Goldstone Properties web application (the “App”). The App is an internal, invite-only tool used by the Company to manage real-estate properties, leads, tasks, financials, and related documents.

Who this applies to

The App is not available to the general public. Access is limited to authorized Company personnel who have been invited by an administrator. This policy describes our data practices for those authorized users and for the business data they manage within the App.

Information we collect

  • Account information — the name and email address used to sign in, and your assigned role (administrator or member).
  • Business data you enter — property records, leads, contacts, tasks, financial figures, and related notes.
  • Connected-service data — when you connect a third-party account (see below), we retrieve only the data needed to display it in the App, such as QuickBooks projects and profit-and-loss figures, or files stored in Microsoft OneDrive / SharePoint.
  • Technical data — standard log and session information generated when you use the App.

How we use information

  • To authenticate you and provide the App’s functionality.
  • To display, organize, and report on your property, financial, and document data.
  • To secure the App and prevent unauthorized access.

We do not sell your information, and we do not use it for advertising.

Intuit QuickBooks data

If you connect QuickBooks Online, we use Intuit’s authorized OAuth process. We request access only to accounting data (the com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting scope). We use QuickBooks data solely to display your company’s projects and profit-and-loss figures inside the App and to let you import those actuals into your financial overview.

  • Access and refresh tokens are stored securely on our server infrastructure and are never exposed to the browser.
  • We retrieve QuickBooks data only when you are viewing it in the App; we do not resell or share it with third parties.
  • You can disconnect QuickBooks at any time, which deletes the stored connection and tokens.

Microsoft OneDrive / SharePoint data

If you sign in with Microsoft to browse files, we use Microsoft’s sign-in to access only the files and folders you choose to view. We do not copy or retain the contents of those files beyond what is needed to display them during your session.

Third-party service providers

We rely on the following providers to operate the App. Each processes data only as needed to provide their service:

  • Supabase — application database and authentication.
  • Vercel — application hosting and serverless functions.
  • Intuit (QuickBooks Online) — accounting data, when connected.
  • Microsoft — OneDrive / SharePoint file access, when connected.

Data retention

We retain business data for as long as your account is active or as needed to operate the App. Connected-service tokens are retained until you disconnect the service or your account is removed.

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), server-side storage of sensitive credentials, and access controls that limit data to authorized users. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your information.

Your choices

  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account data by contacting us.
  • You can disconnect any connected third-party service at any time from within the App.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
